HOW TO BUY

Set up your wallet

Download Metamask or Trustwallet, another Binance Smart Chain-compatible wallet. Make sure it’s connected to the Binance Smart Chain.

Get some BNB

Buy BNB on a CEX (like Coinbase, Bybit or Binance) and transfer it to your wallet.

Go to Pancakeswap

Head over to Pancakeswap and connect your wallet. Paste the official $CATKE contract address.

Swap BNB for $CATKE

Enter the amount of BNB and confirm the transaction. Now you're holding $CATKE.
